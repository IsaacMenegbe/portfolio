About Me

I am Isaac Menegbe, a frontend developer and web designer with over two years of experience in creating responsive and user-centric websites. With a passion for software development, I specialize in building digital solutions that are both functional and visually appealing. My expertise includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, with a strong focus on delivering seamless user experiences. I have contributed to several projects, including F3PS Farm Product, a farm produce price prediction system, Taskmanager, a task management tool, and Search Job Hub3, a job search platform. Currently, I am expanding my knowledge in Node.js, React and Django while continually enhancing my skills to stay at the forefront of web development. I am committed to learning, innovating, and making meaningful contributions to the tech industry.
